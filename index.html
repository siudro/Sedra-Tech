What We Do

RAISE ZONES

We deliver experiences, not lectures. SedraTech is the stage for RAISE at scale: Robotics, Artificial Intelligence, and Space Exploration. Powered by sponsors, free for youth.

RAISE Centre

A Makers’ hub, kids coding drones, youth building real aircrafts, founders prototyping EVs, spaces for AI startups.

ICON FOOTBALL

The world's largest 40-meter football-steel monument, representing Planet Earth and its continents for the 2034 World Cup.

MECHAZILLA SAUDI ARABIA

Will Saudi Arabia be ready to catch the Spacex Starship?

MOBILE UNITS

RAISE EV micro-trucks reach rural schools, 500+ underserved youth.

RAISE VISITS

90-minute STEM adventure packages. Tangible builds, lasting memories, 1K kids per month, free via sponsors.
